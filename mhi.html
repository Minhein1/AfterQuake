Minor Head Injuries

Minor Head Injuries: These include bumps, bruises, or small cuts from impact during an earthquake. They usually heal on their own but require monitoring for serious symptoms.

First Aid: Clean cuts with water and cover with a sterile dressing. Apply a cold compress to reduce swelling and pain.

Watch for confusion, vomiting, drowsiness, or behavior changes. Seek immediate medical help if these occur.

Rest for at least 24 hours. Use acetaminophen for pain, but avoid aspirin or NSAIDs early on.
