Shoulder Strains and Sprains

These injuries happen when shoulder muscles or ligaments are stretched or torn, often from falls or sudden moves during earthquakes.

First Aid Steps:

Rest the shoulder and avoid lifting. Apply ice wrapped in cloth for 15–20 minutes several times daily for 48 hours to ease swelling and pain.

Use a sling to limit movement if needed. Take ibuprofen or acetaminophen for pain and inflammation.

Once pain decreases, start gentle exercises to restore motion. Avoid heavy activities until fully healed.

See a doctor if swelling is severe, there’s numbness, deformity, or loss of movement.
