Discover organizations that offer grants and financial support.

International Committee of the Red Cross (ICRC)

The International Committee of the Red Cross (ICRC) has increasingly adopted Cash Transfer Programming (CTP) as a vital method for providing assistance to people affected by armed conflict and other situations of violence, including earthquake survivors.

Website: https://www.icrc.org/

Medair

Medair provides direct financial aid to crisis-affected people through Multipurpose Cash Assistance. This lets families buy what they urgently need like food, water, or shelter materials, ensuring dignity and supporting local markets.

Website: https://www.medair.org/

Oxfam

Oxfam International's headquarters are in Nairobi, Kenya, but individual Oxfam affiliates have offices and contact points in their respective countries. In Myanmar, Oxfam works with an office in Yangon and has teams deployed to operational areas during emergencies.

Website: https://www.oxfam.org/en/what-we-do/countries/myanmar-burma

Disasters Emergency Committee (DEC)

The DEC brings together 15 leading UK aid charities (including Oxfam, British Red Cross, Save the Children, World Vision, Plan International, and CARE International) to raise funds quickly during humanitarian crises. They have launched a Myanmar Earthquake Appeal. Donations to this appeal will be distributed among their member charities to provide various forms of aid, including cash support.

Website: https://www.dec.org.uk/
